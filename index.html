About me

I’m Mashruf Hossain Rohan, a passionate graphic designer driven by creativity and innovation. With a focus on crafting visually compelling designs, I aim to transform ideas into meaningful visual experiences. Design is my passion, and through my personal brand, RGXD, I strive to inspire and connect through every project I create.

Design isn’t just my profession—it’s my way of storytelling. Every color, line, and detail I create reflects my passion for turning visions into impactful visuals.
